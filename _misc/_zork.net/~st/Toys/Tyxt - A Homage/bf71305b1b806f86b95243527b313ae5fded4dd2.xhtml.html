A tightly-coiled set of stairs nestles between the dock and the rockier extremes of the island - conveniently enough there’s even little night-lights on each step so you can find your way in the dark, although you’re not sure how they’re powered. A little way up, the stairs pause at a sort of paved promenade that runs parallel to the dock, before continuing to wind their way up to a sort of platform built on a huge rocky outcrop. The promenade connects to the rest of island, while the platform looks to contain a pile of enormous gears. The dock extends along a straight stretch of shoreline, with an iron wall on one side and sparkling sea on the other.

You may:

Walk up to the promenade,

Climb up to the giant gears or

Walk out along the dock.
