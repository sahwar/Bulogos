You stand on a circular, concrete-topped platform that was once a rocky islet. Before you, an immense grimy-yellow rocketship lies precariously; its nose and fins extending well past the edges of the platform at both sides. You stifle a momentary urge to give it a shove and see if it will roll off the edge of the platform to its doom.

The rocketship has a single door, which is currently sealed tight.

Return to the causeway.
