Translation team for Bulgarian

The Bulgarian translation team uses bg as language code. This code is usable as the value of the LANGUAGE or LANG environment variable by users of internationalized software. It is also part of PO file names. We often use it as a short identification for the team.

The team uses dict@fsa-bg.org for an official email address, which reaches either a mailing list, or someone who broadcasts information to all other team members. Alexander Shopov currently acts as the team leader, and you may write to him or her for all matters related to team coordination.

The Translation Project registry knows about the following translators for the Bulgarian team.

The Autosend column is for translators who want PO files sent to them on generation, while new POT files are being uploaded to the project. Some translators want both the notice and the file in their mailbox, instead of fetching it through the Web. Just ask if you want this service for yourself.
